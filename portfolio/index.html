WORKFLOW02

Before an idea can become reality, it has to make sense of it. Thinking about the customer's requirements and the user's needs, the two essential ingredients for a successful project.

First Contact
At the initial consultation, we will establish the objectives and scope of the project, possible timelines and budget. I explain an example of how I do my work and, if necessary, we can schedule additional meetings.

Planning
Once the scope of the project has been approved, we will draft a possible roadmap. We will bring all the ideas to light and put them in writing, define the technologies to be used and keep in mind the possible problems and their solutions during the work.

Design, Prototype & Build
To create a great design, I need to really understand the content you are working with and create an experience around it. After understanding this I will move on to the prototyping process, where I will create designs that I will review with the client. When the client gives the go-ahead, we will start with the build but not forgetting the client and that new changes may come up.

Handover, Launch and Ongoing support
Once the project is ready to launch, all the files for its operation will be delivered, plus if necessary I will write any documentation to help. And even if everything is finished you can always contact me in case any problem arises or you need help with your project.
